About FCSD

"Shakhtar" is a Ukrainian football club from the city of Donetsk. Performs in the Premier League of the Championship of Ukraine. Created in May 1936. Winner :

  • Winner of the 2009 UEFA Cup.
  • 4-time winner of the USSR Cup.
  • 14-time champion of Ukraine
  • 13-time winner of the Cup of Ukraine
  • 9-time winner of the Super Cup of Ukraine.

Until 2014, he played home games in Donetsk at the "Donbas Arena" stadium. Due to the war in eastern Ukraine, Shakhtar moved to Kyiv, and since 2014, the training base of the Miners has been the Sviatoshin Olympic complex. In 2014-2016, Shakhtar played most of its home matches in Lviv at Arena Lviv, from February 2017 to March 2020, the club played at Metalist FC in Kharkiv. Since May 2020, Shakhtar's home stadium has been NSC Olimpiyskiy in Kyiv. The contract for the performances of the "Miners" at the "Olimpiyskiy" was signed for three years.
